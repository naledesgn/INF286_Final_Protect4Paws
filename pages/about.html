About Us

Who are we?

Welcome to Protect 4 Paws, where our journey began in 1963 with Lucille Taylor's passion for giving unwanted pets a second chance. As a small shelter in Campbell County, we carry on Lucille's legacy by providing a safe haven for surrendered pets from Northern Kentucky and surrounding areas.

Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure every animal in our care finds a loving home, upholding a No-Kill policy that reflects our commitment to their well-being. Despite our modest size, our impact is significant, thanks to the unwavering support of our community. At Protect 4 Paws, we believe in the power of compassion to make a difference in the lives of animals and the people who love them.

We occupy a small space on a pretty patch of land in Wilder, KY. At the shelter, we typically have a capacity of 5-8 dogs and 15-20 cats and kittens.

What we do?

Our time is fully dedicated to ensuring that every animal brought in is loved and cared for and brought up-to-date on all medical essentials. Our mission is to work to understand the animals in our care and in doing so, find them the perfect home.

We are a non-profit organization running solely on donations, and we wouldn't be here without your help! All monetary donations go directly toward our never-ending medical bills. We are also in constant need of kitty litter, canned dog and cat food, dry cat food, and cleaning supplies!
