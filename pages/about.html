About Us

Welcome to Protect 4 Paws, where our journey began in 1963 with Lucille Taylor's passion for giving unwanted pets a second chance. As a small shelter in Campbell County, we carry on Lucille's legacy by providing a safe haven for surrendered pets from Northern Kentucky and surrounding areas. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure every animal in our care finds a loving home, upholding a No-Kill policy that reflects our commitment to their well-being. Despite our modest size, our impact is significant, thanks to the unwavering support of our community. At Protect 4 Paws, we believe in the power of compassion to make a difference in the lives of animals and the people who love them.



We occupy a small space on a pretty patch of land in Wilder, KY. At the shelter, we typically have a capacity of 5-8 dogs and 15-20 cats and kittens.

Our time is fully dedicated to ensuring that every animal brought in is loved and cared for and brought up-to-date on all medical essentials. Our mission is to work to understand the animals in our care and in doing so, find them the perfect home.



We are a non-profit organization running solely on donations, and we wouldn't be here without your help! All monetary donations go directly toward our never-ending medical bills. We are also in constant need of kitty litter, canned dog and cat food, dry cat food, and cleaning supplies!



We are currently by appointment only! Call or email us to get an appointment set up!

Our animals are given free rein and able to enjoy our lovely little slice of the countryside!

Not looking to adopt but would like to help out? We are accepting donations at our Amazon Wishlist!
