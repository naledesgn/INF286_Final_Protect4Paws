Some featured friends

Mouse

While Mouse is only 3 years old, Mouse has quite a storied past of bullying other cats, surprisingly though, has done really well with most cats here at the shelter after having some space to unwind. However, he is not a huge fan of dogs, and will likely do best in a home without them!

Bosco

Bosco definitely needs to go to home where he has a playmate ready to go! And, Bosco can get along with anyone so long as they are alright with his very generous and gentle kisses and the are ready with never-ending butt-rubs.

Charlie

Charlie could do well on his own with some extra doting pawrents and would likely do best in a home without small children

Penny

Among the crew, Penny is the most ‘outgoing’ of his three brothers- but we are still working on social skills for all four of these fellas! It doesn’t help that their momma, Angel, likes to lurk close by- an expert in helicopter parenting!

Mr. Rogers

Mr. Rogers loves humans and is purrfectly content being scooped up and snuggled at any given moment- aside from when you are trying to intervene in the everyday spats he finds himself in/ starts, but even then he is complacent being handled and taken to his pen for timeout.

Necco Wafer

In the 3 second glimpse you can usually get of Necco Wafer, you can tell that along with being a graceful,fast, and handsome boy, he is also the most apprehensive guy around .
