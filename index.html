We protect your best friends since 1963!

Founded in 1963 by Lucille Taylor who devoted her life to the care and protection of unwanted pets in Campbell County, Protect 4 Paws is a No-Kill animal shelter which carries on her legacy by helping to home the surrendered pets of Northern Kentucky and surrounding areas. We are a very small shelter with a very small team doing all that we can!

Learn more about us!

Protect 4 Paws is a 501(c)(3) no-kill animal shelter in Wilder, Kentucky. We provide a safe, loving environment for each and every animal that walks into our facility. Our mission is to find every animal a good home. We are the only No-Kill animal shelter in the Northern KY Area!
