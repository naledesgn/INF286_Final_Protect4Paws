Protect 4 Paws is a 501(c)(3) no-kill animal shelter in Wilder, Kentucky. We provide a safe, loving environment for each and every animal that walks into our facility.

Our mission is to find every animal a good home.

We are the only No-Kill animal shelter in the Northern KY Area
